About the Website

The website uses the NSE India website to fetch the list of the Nifty 50 companies, get stock prices from for the past 5 years, and analyzes the correlation and spread between the stocks to identify highly correlated pairs that can be traded using a mean reversion strategy. The results are cached to avoid fetching data repeatedly.
